Hello, I'm Megan.

Anthropology - Higher Education - Web Development

As a higher ed web professional, I create compelling web content and user experiences, craft cogent information architecture, and develop effective content strategy to drive institutional growth and success.

As an anthropologist, educator and web developer, I want to further explore how the web can best be utilized in teaching, learning, and higher education, as well as understanding more generally how the web influences people and cultures (and vice versa) in order to drive positive change in the world.

I am seeking opportunities for teaching, consulting, research, learning, and professional growth.
