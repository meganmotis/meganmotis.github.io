Hello, I'm Megan.

Anthropologist - Educator - Web Developer

I love exploring how the web can best be utilized in teaching, learning, and higher education, as well as cultivating a deeper understanding more generally of how the web influences people and cultures (and vice versa) in order to drive positive change in the world. I am always seeking opportunities to teach, consult, research, learn, and facilitate my own and others' professional growth.
